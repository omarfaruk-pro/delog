Batumi Bypass Road

The Department of Highways of the Ministry of Regional Development and Infrastructure of Georgia oversees the construction of the Batumi Bypass Road. This project involves the development of a 14-kilometer, two-lane asphalt road, including 19 bridge crossings, four transport hubs, five tunnels, and 57 water pipes.

The Batumi Bypass Road will connect Mahinjauri, Gantiadi, Kapreshumi, Salibauri, Peria, Mahvilauri, and Helvachauri. The present road runs through tourist and residential neighborhoods, producing traffic congestion and posing issues for international transit vehicles navigating tiny streets. Once completed, the Batumi Bypass Road and the Kobuleti Bypass Road will relieve traffic in the Adjaria resort zone, reducing traffic bottlenecks, lowering the danger of road accidents, and increasing transit freight turnover.

Delog actively contributes to the project by delivering the required goods on time. The project is strategically important for the corporation since it will reduce travel times for its vehicles, increasing service efficiency and quality in the region.
