Quality Control

At Delog, our core mission is continuous improvement. We are committed to delivering the best possible service to our customers. To achieve this, we actively listen to their feedback and use it to refine and enhance our offerings. We strengthen our business model by understanding things from our customers' perspectives. Our Delog Qualification Department plays a vital role in this process, conducting thorough research to gain insights from our current clients and potential customers, ensuring we meet their evolving needs.

Here are some things we do to make sure our service is good

Delog ensures that all aspects of your railway shipment are expertly managed, from booking and documentation to the safe and timely delivery of your cargo. With our in-depth knowledge of international rail routes and logistics, we are your trusted partner for railway shipments

  • After each transportation, customers receive an evaluation form to rate our service.

  • Every three months, our customer relations manager contacts clients to gather feedback on our services, which is used to make improvements based on their suggestions.

  • Our team members actively support each other, fostering a culture of growth and excellence.

  • We recognize outstanding performance through our "Employee of the Month" program.

Now, about improving how we work

At Delog, we aim to support every team member in performing their job to the best of their ability, ensuring we provide our customers with the highest level of service. If someone struggles to do something or is not performing at their best, we plan to help them improve. We want every employee at Delog to succeed and deliver excellent results.

This plan applies to everyone at Delog, but it needs to address issues like poor performance due to lack of attention, intentional mistakes, or chronic absenteeism. These situations are managed separately.

If you would like more details about our plan for improving performance, feel free to speak with the personnel team.
