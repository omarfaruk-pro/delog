History

Delog, founded in 2022, is a rapidly growing freight forwarding company specializing in helping import companies consolidate and transport goods from Asia. With warehouses in Shenzhen, Shanghai, Qingdao, and Hong Kong, Delog serves both Georgian and international markets. In a short time, Delog has established itself as one of the leading players in the regional transportation industry. Numerous top global companies trust Delog for its expertise and commitment to quality. As customer demands increased, the company encountered new challenges, fueling its growth and driving the acquisition of additional financial and human resources. Through its accumulated experience and successful projects, Delog has solidified its position in international shipping. The company quickly expanded, launched new initiatives, and earned a reputation as a responsible and desirable employer.

Corporate strategy

At Delog, we are committed to empowering our customers' success through data-driven decision-making, continuous optimization, and the evolution of supply chain processes. We focus on fostering long-term partnerships, continuously accumulating knowledge and experience, and proactively identifying and mitigating risks. We are dedicated to creating tailored logistics solutions that meet and exceed expectations, supporting businesses of all sizes across diverse sectors, including retail and consumer trade. Through these efforts, we help drive our clients' growth, efficiency, and resilience.

Mission

At Delog, our mission is to deliver optimal logistics solutions backed by a team of highly skilled and motivated professionals. We aim to lead in our field by providing the best shipping services. We prioritize listening to our customers and incorporating their feedback into our operations to achieve this. We strive to strengthen our business model by viewing challenges from our customers' perspective. We conduct in-depth research to understand the needs and opinions of our current and potential clients who have yet to experience our services. Committed to offering innovative, data-driven solutions, we tailor our approach to meet the unique needs of each business. By continuously adapting to evolving market dynamics, Delog ensures cost-effective delivery methods, focusing on risk analysis. We empower our customers to succeed in an increasingly complex global market through ongoing innovation.

Vision

At Delog Logistics, our vision is to be the trusted leader in freight forwarding by providing innovative, reliable, and tailored logistics solutions. Driven by expertise, technology, and a commitment to responsibility, we prioritize transparency, accountability, and ethical integrity in all our relationships. We ensure seamless operations and build lasting trust by listening to our clients and continuously adapting to their needs. We aim to deliver exceptional service, uphold our reputation for reliability, and be the go-to partner for businesses seeking efficient and ethical logistics solutions.

Purpose

Delog is a fast-growing leader in the region, committed to delivering exceptional service, competitive pricing, and comprehensive solutions to our customers. Through the advancement of technology, we can implement cutting-edge methods of automation, optimization, monitoring, and quality control, ensuring the highest standards of transportation in the region.
